<Title>Advance Care Plan Components</Title>
<Subtitle>Health Care Power of Attorney</Subtitle>
<Content><p>If the power of attorney is a "durable" power of attorney this means that the person authorized to make your health care decisions can make them even after you become incapacitated. Having a durable health care power of attorney helps ensure that the specific person you want to make decisions for you, such as a family member, will have the legal right to act on your behalf.</p></Content>
