<Title>Advance Care Planning</Title>
<Subtitle>Changing Your Mind</Subtitle>
<Content><p>It is common for people to change their mind about their medical care. You can always change or cancel advance care documents, provided you are capable (competent). If your wishes change, be sure to let your family and health care team know, so that they can provide you with the type of supportive care that you really want.</p></Content>
