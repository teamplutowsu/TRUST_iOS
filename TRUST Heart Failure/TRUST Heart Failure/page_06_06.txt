<Title>Managing Feelings About Heart Failure</Title>
<Subtitle>Recognizing Depression and Anxiety</Subtitle>
<Content><p>
You may be depressed if you have any of the following symptoms for more than two weeks:<br>
<ul>
<li>Down mood</li>
<li>Feeling blue</li>
<li>Irritability</li>
<li>Loss of interest in things you used to enjoy</li>
<li>Withdrawal from others</li>
<li>Excessive sleepiness</li>
<li>Feeling worthless or guilty</li>
<li>Hopelessness</li>
<li>Suicidal feelings</li>
<li>Preoccupation with death</li>
</ul>
</p></Content>
