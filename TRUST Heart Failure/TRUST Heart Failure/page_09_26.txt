<Title>Advance Care Planning</Title>
<Subtitle>Questions for Your Doctor or Nurse</Subtitle>
<Content><p>You may wish to ask the following questions during your discussion:<br>
<ul><li>Is my heart failure progressing slowly or rapidly?</li>
<li>Is my heart failure still responding to medications?</li>
<li>Is there anything else that can help me, when my heart failure no longer responds to my current therapy?</li>
<li>Are there things I can be doing better or more of to improve the quality and length of my life?</li>
<li>Do you have a palliative medicine team you routinely work with?</li></ul></p></Content>
