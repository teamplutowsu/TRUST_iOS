<Title>Managing Feelings About Heart Failure</Title>
<Subtitle>What You Can Do About Your Feelings</Subtitle>
<Content><p>
There are many things that you can do to eliminate depression and anxiety and improve your quality of life. Try the following:<br>
<ul>
<li>One of the most powerful things you can do to combat depression and anxiety is to talk about these feelings with someone. Sometimes the simple act of saying a problem or worry out loud makes it more manageable and less overwhelming.<br>
Consider talking to a trusted friend, family member, someone else with heart failure, or your doctor or nurse. Your doctor or nurse can also help you explore other treatment options for emotional problems.</li>
</ul>
</p></Content>
