<Title>Self-Care</Title>
<Subtitle>Overcoming Barriers to Treatment</Subtitle>
<Content><p>
Your doctor or nurse can help you overcome the barriers and hurdles you may face in following your treatment plan. For example, they can:<br>
<ul>
<li>See if less expensive medicines are available.</li>
<li>Suggest classes that teach some simple cooking techniques.</li>
<li>Connect you with other heart failure patients who can teach you ways to better manage your treatment plan to make it work for you.</li>
<li>Talk with you about your concerns.</li>
</ul>
</p></Content>
