<Title>Self-Care</Title>
<Subtitle>Early Symptoms of Heart Failure</Subtitle>
<Content><p>
Call your doctor or nurse if you have any of the following symptoms, so they can be treated before they become urgent:<br>
<ul>
<li>A weight gain or loss of 2 or more pounds in 1 day, or 4 pounds in 1 week.</li>
<li>Swelling in the legs, feet, hands, or abdomen.</li>
<li>A persistent cough or chest congestion.</li>
<li>Increasing fatigue or a sudden decrease in your ability to do your usual activities.</li>
<li>A loss of appetite or nausea.</li>
<li>A feeling of fullness or bloating in your stomach.</li>
<li>Confusion or restlessness.</li>
<li>Intermittent or mild shortness of breath.</li>
<li>Dizziness or lightheadedness.</li>
</ul>
</p></Content>
