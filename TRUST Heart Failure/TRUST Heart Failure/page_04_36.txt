<Title>Self-Care</Title>
<Subtitle>Prevent Fatigue from Interfering with Your Life</Subtitle>
<Content><p>
If fatigue continues, try these tips:<br>
<ul>
<li>Plan for adequate sleep and rest periods.
<li>Group similar activities such as carrying items up stairs.
<li>Use energy saving ways of doing your usual activities:<br>
	<ul>
	<li>Do not try to do too much at one time. Schedule things at varying times throughout the day, so you can alternate periods of activity and rest.</li>
	<li>Ask your family and friends for help.</li>
	<li>Sit while you are taking a shower or doing other grooming activities.</li>
	<li>Wear clothes with zippers and front openings to decrease the effort of dressing.</li>
	</ul>
</ul>
</p></Content>
