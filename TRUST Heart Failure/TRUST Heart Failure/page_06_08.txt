<Title>Managing Feelings About Heart Failure</Title>
<Subtitle>Recognizing Depression and Anxiety</Subtitle>
<Content><p>
You may be anxious if you have any of the following symptoms for more than two weeks:<br>
<ul>
<li>Excessive worry</li>
<li>Fear</li>
<li>Tension</li>
<li>Feeling keyed up or always on edge</li>
<li>Restlessness</li>
<li>Feeling shaky</li>
</ul>
</p></Content>
