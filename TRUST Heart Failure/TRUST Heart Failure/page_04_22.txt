<Title>Self-Care</Title>
<Subtitle>Emergency Symptoms of Heart Failure</Subtitle>
<Content><p>
Call 911 for emergency help if you have:<br>
<ul>
<li>Chest discomfort or pain that lasts more than 15 minutes that is not relieved with rest or nitroglycerin.</li>
<li>Severe, persistent shortness of breath.</li>
<li>Fainted or passed out.</li>
</ul>
</p></Content>
