<Title>Self-Care</Title>
<Subtitle>Following Your Treatment Plan </Subtitle>
<Content><p>
Your doctor or nurse will develop a treatment plan that is right for you. Your plan may include recommendations to:<br>
<ul>
<li>Take your medicines exactly as directed.</li>
<li>Weigh yourself every day to see if you are retaining fluid.</li>
<li>Follow a low-sodium diet.</li>
<li>Monitor your symptoms every day.</li>
<li>Avoid alcohol or drink sparingly (less than 2-3 standard drinks/week).*</li>
<li>Get vaccinations such as flu shots routinely.</li>
<li>Control your body weight if you are overweight or have diabetes.</li>
<li>Get regular physical activity.</li>
<li>Quit smoking cigarettes and cigars if you smoke.</li>
<li>Learn when to consult your doctor or nurse.</li>
<li>Make following your treatment plan a permanent part of your life.</li>
</ul>
</p></Content>
