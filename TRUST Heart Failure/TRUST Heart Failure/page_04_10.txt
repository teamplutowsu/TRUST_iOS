<Title>Self-Care</Title>
<Subtitle>Tips for Taking All of Your Medicines as Prescribed</Subtitle>
<Content><p>
<ul>
<li>Use a pillbox marked with each day of the week and the times of day you take your medicines. Fill the pillbox at the beginning of each week. Put it in a convenient place. For example, you can place it on the table where you eat or in another spot you go to every day. If you have trouble filling a pillbox, ask a family member to do it for you. Using a pillbox is one of the best ways to remember to take all of your pills every day.</li>
</ul>
</p></Content>
