<Title>Self-Care</Title>
<Subtitle>Monitor Your Weight and Body Swelling Daily</Subtitle>
<Content><p>
Checking for weight gain is important. You can gain weight without swelling. The average person can hold about 8 to15 extra pounds of fluid before developing swelling. That is why one of the best ways to watch for fluid building up is to weigh yourself every day.<br>
The goal is to recognize a change in weight before you develop swelling and other symptoms. That way you can take steps early to remove the extra weight. 
</p></Content>
