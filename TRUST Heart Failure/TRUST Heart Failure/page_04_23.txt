<Title>Self-Care</Title>
<Subtitle>Urgent Symptoms of Heart Failure</Subtitle>
<Content><p>
Call your doctor immediately if you have any of the following symptoms:<br>
<ul>
<li>Increasing shortness of breath or a new shortness of breath while resting.</li>
<li>Trouble sleeping due to difficulty breathing. For example, waking up suddenly at night due to difficulty breathing.</li>
<li>A need to sleep sitting up or on more pillows than usual.</li>
<li>Fast or irregular heart beats, palpitations, or a “racing heart” that persists and makes you feel dizzy or lightheaded.</li>
Or if you:
<li>Cough up frothy or pink sputum.</li>
<li>Feel like you may pass out.</li>
</ul>
</p></Content>
