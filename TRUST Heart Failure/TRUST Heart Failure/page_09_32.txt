<Title>Making Care Choices</Title>
<Subtitle>Palliative Care</Subtitle>
<Content><p>Palliative care programs are designed to improve the quality of life of patients and their families when they are faced with a life-threatening illness. Since the course of heart failure may not be predictable, this type of program does not have the same entry requirements as a hospice program, which typically requires patients to have a life expectancy of 6 months or less.</p></Content>
