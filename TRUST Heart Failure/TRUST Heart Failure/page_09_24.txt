<Title>Advance Care Planning</Title>
<Subtitle>Getting Started on an Advance Care Plan</Subtitle>
<Content><p>For example, you can trigger a meaningful discussion by saying:<br>
<ul><li>I'd like to talk about what you should do in the event of an emergency, especially one involving a cardiac arrest.</li>
<li>It seems like I am more tired than I have been in the past. I'd like to talk about what can be done to make me feel better.</li>
<li>Let's talk about what we can do to make my life easier.</li>
<li>I'd like to talk about the help we will need as my heart failure worsens.</li>
<li>We should explore what kind of support is available when I can no longer take care of myself.</li></ul></p></Content>
