<Title>Advance Care Planning</Title>
<Subtitle>Care for the Caretakers</Subtitle>
<Content><p>Asking for help from other family members and friends or from home health nurses and aides can help prevent caretakers from feeling overly burdened. Supporting caretakers as they help a family member or friend who has heart failure is an important part of helping families.</p></Content>
