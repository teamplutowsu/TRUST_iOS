<Title>Advance Care Plan Components</Title>
<Subtitle>Advance Care Directive</Subtitle>
<Content><p>You or another person that you specify can provide guidance about your future medical care in a document called an advance care directive. An advance care directive should provide clear evidence of your wishes regarding treatment and can include a living will, a durable health care power of attorney, and a statement about organ donation.</p></Content>
