<Title>Making Care Choices</Title>
<Subtitle>Hospice Care</Subtitle>
<Content><p><ul><li>The patient and family can control and set the agenda for day-to-day care.<br>
<li>A multidisciplinary team that includes nurses, volunteers, chaplains, therapists, bereavement counselors, social workers, and doctors are there for the patient.</li>
<li>Care includes a spiritual component (not specific to one religion or faith) to help patients discuss and reconcile the meaning of end-of-life.</li>
<li>Long-term bereavement support is available for family and friends.</li></ul></p></Content>
